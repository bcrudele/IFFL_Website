Draft Details: Scheduled for June 14th


There are officially less than 1 month until drafting day! This quick post should provided some more insight on how draft day should progress.


This season welcomes 9+ new players to the league! The Illinois Street Hockey League (ISHL) has been around for more than 6 years, but we have little record-keeping to show for it. Roughly each year was a new season. As new problems emerged in the past, as a league we remained flexible. With more options and assistance at the league's disposal, this will be our most comfortable and professional season to date!


The biggest problem in the league's past-time was player availability. The introduction of many passionate players allows me to believe we are set for a great summer. This was made especially made possible by Recruiting Lead and Algonquin Assassins General Manager, Anthony Woodcock. Thank you, Anthony! Creating a balanced draft is the last piece of the puzzle before the season can officially begin. Here is what the plan is as of now:


  • Draft Date: June 14th
  • 7:30 pm: Draft Lottery (only team captains)
  • 7:30 pm - 7:45 pm: Captains continue working on their picks according to the lottery
  • 7:45 pm: Arrive in professional attire - pictures may be taken
  • 7:45 pm - 8:30 pm: Eat food and talk with potential team-mates
  • 8:30 pm - 9:00 pm: Draft sequence, 2-3 minutes between each draft pick
  • 9:00 pm: End of draft; encouraged to eat, chat, and group up with your new team
  • Draft will be held mostly outdoors
  • Players who cannot attend may join Online
  • Skills competition or a draft combine may be created at a future date to show captains the potential of each player
  • ~5 Rounds
  • 4 Picks per Round

If you have any questions, feel free to ask in the Discord Channel
